Greetings, visitors. My name is Ann and this is my website for the gap year bucketlist. The website comprises of list of things such as producing fairy tales, learning how to code, learning how to produce music, and many more. Please feel free to look around and contact me with encouragements and questions.

I would like to become a person of use. I have flaws, but they are to be fixed. If I could be of help, I want to help animals to live a better life. For a long time, I have always wanted to found a cosmetic company.

However, as I adopted my dog, Dokdo, I fell in love with how animals behave and deeply empathized with animals who get maltreatments from humans. Therefore, I long to help them but at the same time, do the thing that excites me. As a result, I came up with vegan cosmetic company. I want to prove people that beauty can be made without taking it away from animals.
